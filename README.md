# 📓 academic projects

A collection of coursework and projects completed during my undergraduate studies in data analytics. Organized by year, these projects span data engineering, statistical modeling, sentiment analysis, econometrics, and applied research.

---

## junior year

### ☁️ AWS cloud data pipeline
> Cloud infrastructure · SQL Server · Power BI

A cloud-based data pipeline built on AWS RDS to deploy a SQL Server environment, load a Greenhouse Gas emissions database, and surface insights through a Power BI dashboard. The dashboard visualizes emissions sources, historical trends, and contributing gases across sectors.

---

### 📈 regression analysis: food prices & taste scores
> Excel · Simple Linear Regression · Descriptive Statistics

A simple linear regression analysis examining the relationship between food taste scores and meal prices across New York City restaurants. Includes descriptive statistics, assumption checks, outlier identification, and full model evaluation.

---

### 📝 IT ethics: the societal impact of generative AI
> Research Essay

An examination of the societal, environmental, and ethical implications of generative AI, covering workforce displacement, copyright infringement, corporate accountability, and the evolving regulatory landscape shaping AI's future.

---

### 🔒 data acquisition & management: the 2013 Target breach
> Case Study · Cybersecurity · Ethics

An in-depth case study of the 2013 Target data breach, tracing the attack timeline, technical vulnerabilities, and third-party risk failures. Draws out the ethical and organizational lessons for modern corporate cybersecurity practice.

---

### 🎵 digital marketing analysis: Spotify
> R · Kaggle · A/B Testing · Presentation

A digital marketing analysis of Spotify examining ecosystem strengths and weaknesses, two A/B tests exploring how release day and genre influence stream counts, and a breakdown of analytical tools used. Built in R using Kaggle data spanning 2015–2025.

---

## senior year

### 🧙 Harry Potter sentiment analysis
> R · NLP · Text Mining · NRC / Bing / AFINN

A text mining project applying NRC, Bing, and AFINN sentiment lexicons to the Harry Potter series. Analyzes emotional tone across books, chapters, and individual words to surface narrative patterns and conflict-driven language shifts.

---

### 🏛️ presidential speech sentiment analysis
> R · NLP · Statistical Analysis

A sentiment analysis of 227 U.S. presidential State of the Union addresses, examining how emotional tone has shifted over time, varied across presidents, and whether party affiliation has a statistically significant effect on speech sentiment.

---

### 📊 GDP & PCE OLS regression analysis
> R · OLS Regression · Econometrics

An OLS regression analysis examining the relationship between U.S. GDP and Personal Consumption Expenditure from 1970 to 2025. Compares a linear model against a log-log specification to determine which better captures long-term economic growth patterns.
